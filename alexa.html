Our Alexa skills are designed to be informative, calming, and screen-free, well suited for voice-first learning.

🟢 Science Highlight (Live)

Say “Alexa, open Science Highlight” to hear a short, spoken feature on a recent science breakthrough. Each entry is hand-picked for accuracy and clarity, followed by a simple quiz to reinforce learning.

How to Use It

You can say:

  • “1”, “2”, or “3” to answer the quiz
  • “Next” to hear the next entry
  • “Stop” to exit at any time
