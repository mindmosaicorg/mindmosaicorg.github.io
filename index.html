Welcome to Mind Mosaic

Mind Mosaic is a voice-first, screen-light project dedicated to lifelong learning. We create simple tools for listening, reflection, and understanding, especially for those who are vision-challenged or seek to reduce screen time.

Our content is hand-curated, science-grounded, and never gimmicky. Everything we publish is crafted with clarity and care, and freely offered through Alexa and Substack.
