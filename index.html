Mind Mosaic: Science Milestones

Mind Mosaic: Science Milestones is an Alexa skill that shares one important scientific breakthrough from the 2020s every time you listen. From biology and medicine to climate science, space exploration, robotics, quantum physics, and computing — each insight is curated to spark wonder and lifelong learning.

How to Use It

Say: “Alexa, open Mind Mosaic”

Then say:

  • “1”, “2”, or “3” to answer the quiz
  • “Next” to hear the next entry
  • “Stop” to exit at any time
